About

Hi, I'm Fadex.
I edit videos.

I'm a 21-year-old video editor from Sri Lanka with over 5 years of experience. I started with CapCut and Filmora and now work exclusively in DaVinci Resolve.

I've worked on everything from long-form YouTube content to short-form edits and cinematic montages. Every project I focus on clean cuts, good pacing, and storytelling that keeps viewers watching.

FYI — I am not an Advanced Motion Designer. I specialise in clean cuts, pacing and storytelling — not heavy motion graphics.
